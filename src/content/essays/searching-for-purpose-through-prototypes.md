## Searching for Purpose Through Prototypes

Every prototype is a question with a deadline.

Some questions are technical: can this work, can this scale, can this be deployed before judging starts? Others are personal: do I care enough to keep going, does this problem matter, who am I becoming through the things I keep choosing to build?

The answer rarely arrives fully formed.

It appears through motion, through repeated attempts, through the quiet record of what survives after demo day.
