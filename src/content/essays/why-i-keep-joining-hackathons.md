## Why I Keep Joining Hackathons

Hackathons compress the distance between who you think you are and how you actually build.

In normal time, ambition can hide behind planning. In 48 hours, the idea has to become a thing. The team has to decide. The prototype has to work. The story has to land.

I keep joining because the pressure reveals what I still need to learn.

## The Honest Clock

A deadline does not care how elegant the architecture is. It asks a simpler question: can someone use this before the demo?

That question has changed how I build.
