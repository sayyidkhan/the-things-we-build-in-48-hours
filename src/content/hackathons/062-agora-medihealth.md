## Summary

A healthcare coordination prototype built around clearer intake, triage, and follow-up.

## What Happened

The project forced the team to slow down and make every screen explain itself without hiding uncertainty.

## What Worked

Simple flows, plain language, and visible next steps made the prototype feel credible.

## What Broke

The original workflow had too many branches for a 48-hour build.

## Lesson

When the domain is sensitive, the interface has to earn trust before it asks for action.
