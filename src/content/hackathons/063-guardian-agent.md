## Summary

An agentic voicemail concept that acts as a proxy when a person is unavailable, gathering context and responding with care.

## What Happened

The build explored the tension between automation and trust: what should an agent decide, and what should it only collect?

## What Worked

The premise was immediately understandable because everyone has missed calls, unfinished context, and moments where they need a proxy.

## What Broke

The hard part was not the interface. The hard part was knowing when the agent should stop.

## Lesson

The best agent products are not just capable. They are restrained.
