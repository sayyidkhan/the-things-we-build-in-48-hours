## Summary

A tower defense prototype built in 48 hours, shaped by the pressure of making a complex system legible before demo time.

## What Happened

The team started with a larger simulation and kept cutting until the loop was understandable: defend, upgrade, survive, repeat.

## What Worked

The clearest parts were the ones we could explain in a sentence and show on screen without narration.

## What Broke

Balance, pacing, and onboarding all competed for the same final hours.

## Lesson

Ship the smallest complete loop first. Complexity can return after the demo works.
